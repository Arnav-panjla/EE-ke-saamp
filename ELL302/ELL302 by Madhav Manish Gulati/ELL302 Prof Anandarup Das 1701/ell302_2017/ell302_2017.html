ELL302 Power Electronics

General information

ELL302-Power Electronics (3-0-0). Mon, Thurs: 9.30-11 am, LH 410.

Course outline

  • Introduction and application of Power Electronics

  • Power Devices and their properties: Types of Power devices and their comparison, losses and thermal modelling, gate drivers, Wide Band Gap devices.

  • DC-DC converters: Buck, boost, buck-boost, isolated converters. Analysis of converters. PWM control and operation. Applications.

  • DC-AC converters: Inverter circuit configurations and principle of operation, single and three-phase, configurations, Square wave and sinusoidal PWM control methods and harmonic control. Applications.

  • AC-DC converters: Uncontrolled, semi-controlled and fully controlled converters in single-phase and three-phase configurations, Phase control, harmonic analysis, firing circuits. Improved power quality AC-DC converters. Applications.

  • AC-AC converters: Introduction to AC to AC conversion, single phase and three-phase AC voltage controller circuit configurations, applications, advantages, harmonic analysis.

  • Power Electronic systems

Attendance is compulsory. If you have 100% attendance, you will get 3 bonus marks. If you have less than 75% attendance, you get 1 grade less. If you have less than 60% attendance, you get 2 grades less. Less than 50% attendance, you get an E/F grade.
Quizzes will not be repeated under any circumstance.
